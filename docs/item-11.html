Once I knew one person who said to me: 'I can't understand you. What do you need?'. I answered: 'A good conversation'. But it was easier for the person: to buy a present. What should I do with the presents? I can put it on my shelf, I can say 'thanks' but it's not the point about relations. The point - is a f***ing deep and great talk when one theme slowly flows into another and it's already night.
