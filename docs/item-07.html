time and gravity

We are all in a big problem: we still don't know how to unite quantum mechanics and relativity theory. Let's take less - we still can't find solutions for earth pollution and global warming. I mean, we know what to do but we don't do it. I still can't understand why we need countries and borders - we all are the one species on the planet Earth. We should think about it and stop doing this shit.
