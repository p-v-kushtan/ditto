I want to kiss you. I could.

Because I like you,

and we feel so good together,

and you are so beautiful.

So

I won't.

Because all of this will

disappear

We'll miss it after crossed the line.

All this world around us will be destroyed.

I want to keep it.

Because I care. Because I want.

And because of it - I won't.
