welcome

Why art? What’s the point to create and to share? To reduce the amount of loneliness. I may stumble upon a book by an unknown author, read a couple of lines and exclaim: ‘Gosh! I feel the same!’. And it seems to me that this alone is worth sharing your art. For the sake of an act of humanity.
