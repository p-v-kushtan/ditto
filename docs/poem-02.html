What IS the rest?

I feel it when I work

I feel it when I’m tired

I even feel it when I’m sad

And find myself in wish of dying.

What IS the rest?

The state of thoughtless?

Or more -

the state of stone in heart?

For me - the rest

is all our survey

all our trip

and all our journey.

For the best of each of us.

But what’s the BEST?

I am a poet - Do I know?

Do you know

what IS it?

The every day?

The every winning?

The every act of love

with the one you love?

What is the best?

The same as rest

- it’s nothing and it’s all.

I know the best

I know the rest

And know - that’s all the same.
